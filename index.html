About

Not your typical engineer.

Before writing code professionally, I spent years on Chicago's trading floors building pricing models and managing risk in high-stakes, real-time environments. That chapter taught me things no CS curriculum covers: how to stay calm under pressure, build things that actually have to work, and know when to raise your hand.

I eventually went back for an M.S. in Computer Science at UChicago, and since then I've built backend systems across healthcare, data infrastructure, and e-commerce. I care about clean architecture, high test coverage, and actually shipping things on time.

I work best as part of a team. I ask questions, take feedback seriously, and try to leave things better than I found them.
